## **GAMEPLAY CHANGES**

* Using one artillery disables the others until the cooldown finishes
* Fixed being able to activate multiple artillery strikes at the same time
* Banzai charging reduces damage up to 95%, starting at 70% and scales upwards
* You can no longer be slowed when shot in the leg during a Banzai Charge
* Fixed HE projectiles for various Tanks
* Focus zoom affects weapon FOV

### **Weapons**

* Fixed g43 scope pickup causing server crashes
* Fixed arisaka 99 having the wrong iron sight fov
* Fixed Gunto (Katana) not spawning on TL
* Disabled misalignment for the Type 100/40
* Lewis / Type 92 lmg can now be braced
* PPS42 buffed, increased stability
* Barrel bursting works, turns your MG semi-auto
* Type 97 grenade traps blow up instantly on trigger, and show up on your hud when placed
* PzB 39 is now capable of penetrating the T-34's weak spots
